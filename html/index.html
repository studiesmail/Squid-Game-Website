ABOUT

"Squid Game" is a South Korean survival drama series about 456 deeply indebted individuals who compete in a series of deadly children's games for a massive cash prize. The series, created by Hwang Dong-hyuk, explores themes of desperation, social inequality, and the corrupting influence of wealth

The Premise:

The series revolves around Seong Gi-hun, a financially struggling man who is invited to participate in a mysterious competition. He and 455 other players, all facing dire financial circumstances, are lured into playing deadly versions of classic Korean children's games.

Dalgona challenge, players must carefully carve out a shape from a brittle honeycomb candy (Dalgona) using a needle, without breaking the candy or taking too long. Failure to do so results in elimination (death). The shapes include a circle, triangle, star, and umbrella, with the triangle being considered the easiest and the umbrella the most difficult
